02 // Core Capabilities

What I Actually Do.

Five capabilities. Each one delivers measurable improvement in decision speed, financial resilience, and organizational durability.

01

Turn Strategy into an Operating System

Most strategies fail at translation. Boards approve plans, but execution drifts. I design the connective tissue:

  • → Decision rights frameworks — who decides what, when, with what data
  • → Execution rhythms — weekly/monthly/quarterly cadences that force accountability
  • → KPI architectures — leading indicators that predict problems before they metastasize
  • → Governance models — board oversight without micromanagement

Deliverable: A business that executes strategy without constant CEO intervention.

02

Fix Broken Decision-Making

Boards make bad decisions for three reasons: wrong data, wrong timing, wrong people in the room.

I install decision infrastructure:

  • → Data quality protocols — eliminate garbage-in-garbage-out
  • → Decision triggers — automated alerts when thresholds breach
  • → Risk frameworks — quantify downside before committing capital

Deliverable: Decisions made in days, not months. With 80% less emotional friction.

03

Engineer Financial Strength

Growth without financial discipline is suicide. I've seen businesses with $50M revenue collapse because they couldn't manage $2M in working capital.

I build financial control systems:

  • → 13-week cash flow models — predict liquidity crises before they happen
  • → Working capital optimization — free up trapped cash in inventory/receivables
  • → Cost structure redesign — convert fixed costs to variable where possible
  • → Capital allocation frameworks — ROI hurdles that prevent value destruction

Deliverable: Financial resilience that survives market shocks and leadership transitions.

04

Build Future-Ready Organizations

Most businesses optimize for today and get blindsided by tomorrow. I design dual operating systems:

  • → Horizon scanning processes — systematic monitoring of emerging threats/opportunities
  • → Innovation budgets — ring-fenced capital for experimentation
  • → Scenario planning frameworks — stress-test strategy against multiple futures
  • → Learning systems — capture and institutionalize what works

Deliverable: Organizations that adapt faster than their markets decay.

05

Develop Thinking Leaders

Systems fail when people don't understand them. I develop executives who can diagnose, not just execute:

  • → Systems thinking — see second-order effects before making decisions
  • → Financial fluency — CFO-level understanding for non-finance executives
  • → Strategic diagnosis — identify root causes, not symptoms
  • → Decision discipline — frameworks that prevent emotional/political decisions

Deliverable: Leadership teams that don't need you in every meeting. Custom Mini-MBA programs, CFO mastery tracks, board workshops.
